My name is Longyuan Du. I was born and raised in Shanghai, China. In 2010 after college, I moved to US, first as a graduate student and later as a professional. I quit my job in 2014 and went back to school. I am currently a PhD student at Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto.

I first started hiking in the winter of 2010. I did the classic W route of Torres del Paine. Since then, I have hiked in US, Canada, Nepal, China, Australia and Peru. I am constantly looking for new challenges. See you on trail!
